About me

I am Tatyana Shkutina, I was born in the city of Khabarovsk in Russia on January 29, 1952 and I still live there today. I met my husband Dimitri Shkutin when we were children and we have been inseparable ever since! We had 2 children, Marina and Jenia, and we really liked going on trips as a whole family together. Marina immigrated to Israel in 2000 and since then I have visited Israel 3 times, I love the country very much and especially the city of Jerusalem. I have many hobbies, I like to sew, knit, make funny dolls and give them to people I love. In addition, in the summer I like to go to my dacha (summer house) to plant, take care of plants and enjoy nature.
